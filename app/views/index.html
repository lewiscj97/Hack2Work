RouteToWork

This project helps customers find jobs that are more relevant to them than the suggestions currently given by government services. It does this by not only taking into account their skillset, but also considering the job market in their area as well as suggesting courses and upskill opportunities.

Landing point

This app will fit into the user journey after the NCS skills assessment. Users will take the assessment, and we'll use the data they input there as well as their postcode to create more accurate job and learning opportunities than the ones currently suggested to them.
